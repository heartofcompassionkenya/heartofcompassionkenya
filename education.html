Support a Child, Transform a Life

Each child listed below carries a story of hope, resilience, and untapped potential. We invite you to take a moment to read through their profiles and consider becoming a sponsor. By choosing to support one child, you're opening the door to education, health, and a brighter future. Your generosity can change a life.

Thank you for being part of this journey of compassion and impact.

Daniel, Grade 2 – Roysambu, Wetemere Slums

Daniel* is a bright and curious boy in Grade 2 who dreams of becoming a teacher. He lives with his mother in the Wetemere slums of Roysambu, where daily survival is a challenge. As a single mother with no stable income, she struggles to provide school fees, uniforms, and meals for Daniel.

She hopes to start a small tailoring business and is seeking a sewing machine and basic supplies. With your support, Daniel can stay in school, and his mother can build a sustainable way to support her family.

*Name changed for privacy.

Job, Playgroup – Roysambu, Wetemere Slums

Job* is a joyful little boy in playgroup who loves to sing and explore. He lives in the Wetemere slums of Roysambu with his mother, who earns a small income by doing laundry for neighbors. Though she has a husband, their marriage is unstable, and she often carries the full burden of raising Job alone.

Job’s mother is deeply committed to her son’s future but struggles to afford school fees, uniforms, books, meals, and other essentials. She dreams of learning hairdressing—a skill that could help her build a stable income and support her child more consistently.

She is appealing to well-wishers for support with both Job’s education and her empowerment journey. Your kindness can help Job stay in school and give his mother the tools to transform their lives.

*Name changed for privacy.

Catherine’s Children – PP1 & Playgroup, Roysambu, Wetemere Slums

Catherine* is a single mother of three children living in the Wetemere slums of Roysambu. Two of her children should be in PP1 and one in Playgroup, but she has been unable to enroll them in school due to financial hardship. Her small business selling boiled eggs provides only a minimal income, leaving her unable to afford school fees, uniforms, books, meals, and other essentials.

Catherine dreams of acquiring skills in hairdressing and beauty so she can build a more stable livelihood and support her children’s education. She is appealing to well-wishers for help—both in educating her children and in sponsoring her training.

Your support can open the door to learning for her children and empower Catherine to transform their future.

*Name changed for privacy.
