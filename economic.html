Economic Support: Empowering Families to Thrive

In the Wetemere Slums of Roysambu, many parents—especially single mothers—are eager to work but lack the tools, training, or capital to start a business or secure stable income. Without economic opportunity, families remain trapped in a cycle of poverty, and children are often forced to stay out of school.

Economic Support is about breaking that cycle. By equipping caregivers with practical skills and startup resources, we empower them to build sustainable livelihoods, restore dignity, and support their children’s education and well-being.

Your support can help provide:

  • Vocational training in tailoring, catering, hairdressing, beauty, and other marketable skills
  • Startup kits such as sewing machines, salon tools, or small business supplies
  • Mentorship and coaching to help families grow their income and confidence

When a parent is empowered, a child is uplifted—and a community begins to transform.

Partner with us to create lasting change through economic empowerment. Together, we can turn resilience into opportunity.
