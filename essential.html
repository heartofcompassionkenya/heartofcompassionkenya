Essential Support: Restoring Dignity, One Family at a Time

In the Wetemere Slums of Roysambu, families face daily hardship that goes far beyond school fees. Children attend school hungry, without proper clothing, shoes, or basic supplies. Mothers struggle to provide sanitary essentials, and some families lack even the most basic food items or diapers for their young ones.

Essential Support is about meeting these urgent needs with compassion and dignity. It’s about ensuring that no child is held back simply because they lack a uniform, a meal, or clean clothing.

Your contribution—whether financial or in-kind—can make a powerful difference. We welcome:

  • Food items to nourish growing children
  • Clothing and shoes to restore comfort and confidence
  • School supplies to fuel learning and creativity
  • Sanitary essentials and diapers for hygiene and dignity
  • Monthly donations (as little as $10) to sustain support for the most vulnerable

Every gift helps restore hope and stability to families who need it most. Together, we can ensure that every child and caregiver feels seen, supported, and empowered.

Join us in providing the essentials that make life livable—and learning possible.
