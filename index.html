Who We Are

Heart of Compassion Kenya (HOCK), headquartered in Roysambu Nairobi, is a children, youth and women focused CBO. Driven by: "A resilient, empowered community where every child, youth, and woman has access to education, economic opportunities, and holistic well-being" as our vision, HOCK is on a mission to empower vulnerable children, youth, and women through education, essential support, and economic opportunities, while promoting inclusive community development.

Psycho-Social Support

Counseling, trauma healing, and mentorship for street youth, school dropouts, and vulnerable mothers.

Vocational Training

Hairdressing, catering, beauty therapy, and entrepreneurship to build sustainable livelihoods.

Youth Empowerment

Income-generating initiatives like the Trolley Project, SHOFCO training, and GBV awareness programs.
