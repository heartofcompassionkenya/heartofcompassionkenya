Who We Are

Heart of Compassion Kenya (HOCK), headquartered in Roysambu Nairobi, is a youth-focused CBO that seeks to empower youth from informal settlements in Nairobi, Kiambu, Machakos, and beyond Kenya. Driven by: "A thriving community with productive, independent, and transformed youth" as our vision, FOT is on a mission to transform lives through training, mentorship, and empowerment programs.

Learn More

Psycho-Social Support

Counseling, trauma healing, and mentorship for street youth, school dropouts, and vulnerable mothers.

Vocational Training

Hairdressing, catering, beauty therapy, and entrepreneurship to build sustainable livelihoods.

Youth Empowerment

Income-generating initiatives like the Trolley Project, SHOFCO training, and GBV awareness programs.
