Our Programs & Impact

Rescue, Rehabilitation & Psychosocial Support

  • Rescue and rehabilitate vulnerable youth.
  • Provide vocational skills in partnership with organizations (catering, hairdressing, beauty).
  • Distribute sanitary towels to girls in schools and the community (target: 200 pads per school/community outreach).
  • Offer life skills training in schools and churches to empower 1,000 youth/young mothers annually through mentorship programs.
  • Youth employment and entrepreneurship training.
  • Family planning education for young mothers and young adults.
  • Distribute care packages (pads, hygiene items) to vulnerable youth in partnership with schools and institutions.
  • Provide daily hot meals to vulnerable young people and children.
  • Distribute foodstuffs to vulnerable families in the community.
  • Support young mothers returning to school (primary and secondary).
  • Support children of vulnerable young mothers with uniforms and school fees.
  • Provide day care services for children of young mothers.

Establishment of Learning Institutions & Rehabilitation Centers

  • ECDE & Day Care Centers for children of young mothers.
  • Pre-school centers for vulnerable children.
  • Rescue centers for vulnerable children.
  • Rehabilitation centers for vulnerable young adults/youth.
  • Homes for the homeless.

Advocacy for Rights & Inclusion of Young People

  • Promote the rights of young people and ensure their inclusion in community development programs and decision-making.

Impact Highlights

  • Rescued and rehabilitated 10+ young mothers with shelter, training, and support.
  • 172 youth (119 boys, 53 girls) reached with psycho-social support.
  • 176 mothers trained in business skills; 80+ received care packages.
  • Successful reintegration of boys from the street into employment and family life.
  • Creation of small businesses through the Trolley Project and beauty services.
